About me

Who I am

Hello, my name is Mayra Lopez. I am a Full Stack developer in Denver, Colorado. I have learned numerous programming languages. My interest in developing engaging user interfaces is what drove me to specialise in Full Stack development.

What I do

I offer frontend and backend solutions. Scroll down to my portfolio to view some of my work.

Background

  • Coding:Browser Base Technologies I use; HTML5, CSS3, Responsive Design, CSS Frameworks(Bootstrap, Materialize), JavaScript, jQuery, Handlebars, Cookies, Local Storage, React.js. For Deployment I have experience with, Heroku, Git, and Github Pages. PHP Laravel. For Database I use Firebase, MySQL, and MongoDB. For the Server side development I use; Express, Secure and Session Storage, User Authentication, and MERN Stack. I do quality assurance by writing tests.(samples on GitHub).
